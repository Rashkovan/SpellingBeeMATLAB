# SpellingBeeMATLAB
For our CLPS950 group project, we attempted to create a game that was a crossover between Spelling Bee and Hangman. 
For our repo, the main aspect of the game is making a valid English word betwen 4 and 7 letters that followed the 
theme of animal and used a mandatory center letter. You must follow the rules of creating a valid word or lose a life, 
and you lose the game if you lose all your lives. In order to win, you must completely fill the bee with color, or in 
other words, gain enough points. 

We have created a UI that lists the rules of the game, tracks the words created along with the players' scores & points, a 
bee design that acts as a the word counter system, and a game over screen depending on win or loss. Additonally, we 
implemented a word validation feature, where players receive feedback if their submitted word fails to meet the rules of the
game. This feature pulls from a word list we created with all the possible combinations of letters as well as a themed list 
with all words from the word list that follow our theme. It also comes with a penalty counter where players will "lose a life"
if their word does not fulfill the conditions of the word validiation feature.
